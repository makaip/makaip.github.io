my work

Max Planck

June 2025—Present

At the Max Planck Florida Institute For Neuroscience, I work in the Tian Lab, using generalized linear models, long-short term memory, and convolutional neural networks to quantify relationships between fiber-photometry-recorded 5-HT measurements and fear responses in the VTA and OFC. I am currently developing tools to automate behavioral analysis and reproject animal movement.


Harbor Branch

June 2024—Present

In the Geochemical Remote Sensing Lab at the Harbor Branch Oceanographic Institute, I develop machine learning approaches to quantify sediment-derrived dissolved organic carbon (DOC) in the Northern Gulf of Mexico (NGOM) using remote sensing (RS). We use colored dissolved organic matter (CDOM) to locate DOC, and use features such as Chlorophyll-a (Chl-a) and sea surface temperature (SST) to distinguish its source.


URIKA [bioworks]

June 2024—November 2024

I created AWS Neptune's first data processing pipeline for NLP-derived biomedical relational databases using Python, Bash, and SPARQL.
